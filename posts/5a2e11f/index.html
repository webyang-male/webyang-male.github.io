【写作真题】：迎接挑战

【参考范文 1 】 ：

The 21st century has witnessed dramatic changes, bringing about fresh challenges to be
solved every day. For students, meeting challenges is the new normal when they enter the
society. Therefore, we must develop adequate abilities to deal with challenges.
Firstly, when meeting challenges, students have the ability to address them effectively.
For example, they try to improve themselves by working harder, so as not to be defeated
once the challenges appear in their life. Moreover, challenge is a vital source of inspiration
and creative power. If you find something challenging and you’ve decided to face it, you are
determined to devote all your energies to collecting new information in order to accomplish
the task. During this process, fresh knowledge and innovative ideas will come to mind
constantly, which will definitely freshen up your perception. Thus, the ability to meet
challenges can be recognized as a contributing factor to a creative mind.
In conclusion, we students should cultivate our abilities to meet various challenges of
society so that we will better adapt to the rapid social development, and the ability will also
exert an ever-lasting influence upon our future study.
【参考范文 2 】 ：
Develop Our Ability to Meet Challenges
Obviously, in this highly competitive society, fostering students’ ability to meet
challenges is gaining greater importance.
First of all, challenges are inevitable in life. Due to the quickening pace of the current
society, college students are being exposed to diverse kinds of challenges and pressures.
Hence, students are expected to equip themselves with the ability to cope with challenges.
Otherwise, it will be difficult for them to gain a foothold after entering the society. Secondly,
challenges are opportunities to grow. If students can learn to face challenges—even if they
end in failure—with full presence and awareness, they will find most challenges are not
challenges at all, but opportunities to get a glimpse of the highest self. Every challenge they
successfully confront in life serves to strengthen their will, confidence and ability to conquer
future obstacles. Thirdly, cultivating the ability to meet challenges can prepare students
mentally for confronting adversity head-on. Once they’re ready for the worst, we believe
that no trauma will bring them down.
To conclude, developing the ability to confront challenges will benefit students in all
aspects of life.

As contemporary students have been involved in intensified competitions, it is
necessary for them to strengthen a variety of competencies, the most important of which is
effective communication skills.
One of the main reasons is that delivering ideas straightforward and eliminating
misunderstandings are essential, especially for those living in the complicated social
network. Thus, better interpersonal relationships, resulting from the effective
communication skills, will lay a solid foundation for student learning. Another factor
contributing to the necessity of the skills is that students with clear words and gestures can
make new friends with ease. Last but not the least, those abilities must pave the way for
students’ careers in the future. Given that corporations need all-round talents,
communicating well with colleagues, customers and even bosses should be taken into
consideration, so students could cultivate those basic skills in the early stage of their life.
In brief, only with high capacity to communicate effectively can students cope with the
ubiquitous challenges and make great preparations for their future
【参考范文 2 】 ：
Encourage Students to Develop Effective Communication Skills
In today’s society, the importance of cultivating good communication skills cannot be
overstated. And students, as a member of society, it’s vital for them to develop effective
skills to communicate with others.
First of all, a person with effective communication skills finds it easier to put forth his
ideas clearly. In this way some misunderstanding can be effectively avoided, which helps to
develop better interpersonal relationships with people around him. Moreover, a good
communicator is often an excellent mediator and negotiator who is skilled in resolving
disputes and enhancing cooperation. Outstanding communication skills enable him to work
more effectively in groups and teams. Besides, effective communication skills are essential
to developing other key life skills like the problem-solving skill and the decision-making skill
that inevitably occur both in our private and professional lives.
There is a good saying that what communication does for an organization resembles
what the bloodstream does for an organism. Therefore, whether you’re an effective
communicator or not, it’s essential to understand the importance of good communication
and try to improve your communication skills.
